# Hybrid Positioning

For many customers, VMware to Hyper-V is not the end state. It is a controlled transition point that preserves strategic flexibility.

## Positioning Narrative for Partners

- Immediate objective: reduce VMware dependency with minimal disruption
- Mid-term objective: stabilize workloads on Hyper-V
- Strategic objective: create optionality for hybrid and Azure-aligned modernization

## How to Use This in Presales

- Lead with business continuity and risk-managed migration
- Show phased execution rather than all-at-once transformation
- Tie migration outcomes to future infrastructure choices

## Suggested Conversation Framework

1. Why now: licensing and cost pressure
2. What now: controlled migration to Hyper-V
3. What next: hybrid architecture choices based on workload suitability

## Practical Outcome

This playbook helps partners deliver a credible first step: a documented and controlled VMware exit motion with room for future cloud strategy.
